About

Welcome to Out-N-About Sligo,

Sligo, meaning 'abounding in shells' is a coastal seaport and the county town of County Sligo, Ireland. The county is reputed to have the richest concentration of prehistoric monuments in Western Europe, with over 5,000 archaeological sites, one of which is 'Carrowmore', the largest megalithic cemetery in Ireland. Situated on the Wild Atlantic Way, with the coast to the west and lakelands to the east, Sligo town is the gateway to beautiful Irish experiences.

Nevertheless, two mountains surround it, the majestic Benbulben to the east north and the ancient Knocknarea to the West on the coast of the Atlantic Ocean. It is one of the most picturesque landscapes of the country.

As one of the main towns in the West of Ireland it is home to a wide range of attractions. From Outdoor activities, like surfing, walking, hiking or swimming, to amazing Food, lots of happy events and activities. Of course there is also the kindness and banter of people, who make it worth to visit even more.

We want to share all that with you, so keep an eye out for new posts and features.
